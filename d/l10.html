L10 Dashboard — Guide

Purpose

A quick-glance dashboard for the IR team's L10 meeting. Shows quarterly capital raise performance with a comparison indicator, plus recent activity in a configurable rolling window.

Quarter vs. Quarter

Total Raise: Total funded capital (from HubSpot Investment records, anchored on funds_received date) for the current quarter. The delta shows the change vs. the previous quarter's same-point-in-quarter figure.

New Money: Capital from investments classified as "New" (type_of_investment = New).

Repeat Money: Capital from Repeat and Bolt-On investments.

The comparison indicator (↑/↓/—) and percentage show how the current quarter compares to the previous quarter at the same elapsed point. For example, if today is Day 35 of Q3, the comparison is against the first 35 days of Q2.

Recent Activity

Discovery Calls Set: Meetings with call_and_meeting_type = "Discovery Call - Main" or "Discovery Call - Setters" created in the window. Uses meeting creation date (when booked).

Commitments Made: Deals entering the InvestNext commit pipeline stage in the window. Shows count and total dollar amount.

Investments Funded: Investment records with funds_received in the window. Shows count and total dollar amount.

Data Sources

Investments and meetings from the nightly R2 cache (rebuilt 2 AM ET). Commitments fetched live from HubSpot. Use ↻ Refresh to reload.
