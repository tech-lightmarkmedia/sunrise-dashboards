No passive income yet? The benefit still builds.

The losses don't expire — they bank in your name and wait for income to absorb.

01

Generate the loss

Cost segregation plus 100% bonus depreciation turns a large share of your investment into a first-year paper loss on your K-1.

02

Bank it

If you can't use it this year, it isn't lost — it carries forward indefinitely as a suspended passive loss.

03

Absorb income later

It offsets future passive income from any source, and at a full taxable exit the remaining shield releases in one year.
