Campaign ROI Dashboard — Guide

What this dashboard shows

Full-funnel ROI from lead capture through funded investment, per campaign. Every contact ever created by SCI is the universe; their investments are followed forward with no date cap, so a lead from 2024 whose investment funded in 2026 appears in the 2024 cohort.

Data sources

Contacts, investments, and meetings are read from the nightly R2 cache (rebuilt at 2 AM ET). Spend data is fetched live from the Meta and LinkedIn APIs on each load — this is the only non-cached data source and accounts for most of the load time.

Cohort filter

The Cohort dropdown selects which contacts form the cohort based on their createdate in HubSpot. "All Time" includes every contact since 2023. Other ranges filter by creation date. Investments are always followed forward regardless of cohort range.

Source bucket toggle (All / Paid / Organic / Direct)

Filters the table to contacts whose hs_analytics_source falls in the selected bucket. Paid = any source containing "Paid". Direct = "Direct Traffic". Organic = everything else.

Conversion event toggle

All Events groups rows by campaign (DD2 attribution). First Conversion groups by the contact's first_conversion_event_name. Recent Conversion groups by recent_conversion_event_name. In conversion mode, booking and spend columns are hidden.

Table columns

Leads: Total contacts in the cohort attributed to this campaign.

AI Leads: Accredited investor contacts (are_you_accredited_ = "Yes").

CPAIL: Cost Per AI Lead = Spend ÷ AI Leads.

Intros / Cost per Intro: Intro meetings booked by contacts in this campaign's cohort.

Discoveries / Cost per Discovery: Discovery meetings booked by contacts in this campaign's cohort.

Investments: Count of funded investments by contacts in this campaign's cohort (all-time, follow-forward).

Capital Raised: Total dollar amount of those investments.

Cost of Capital: Spend ÷ Investments.

Drill-down

Click any row to see individual contacts and their investments. Each contact shows name (linked to HubSpot), created date, accreditation status, conversion event, investment count, and capital raised. Investments are expanded inline showing fund, type, and date.

Attribution

First-touch campaign attribution using the contact's hs_analytics_source_data_2 (DD2), falling back to DD1 then Original Source. This matches the main Ad Performance dashboard's attribution logic.
