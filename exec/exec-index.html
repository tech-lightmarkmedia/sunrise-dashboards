Forecasting · scenario planning

Predictive Capital Raising

Model the future with real conversion data.
  • Full-funnel sliders: ad spend, AI lead rate, intro booking, discovery conversion, average investment
  • Reinvestment kernel model (v3.0): 60-month empirical curve calibrated from 917 investors — repeat capital lags correctly
  • Two-component repeat: base run-rate from existing investor base + new-investor repeat from the kernel
  • Forward and reverse modes: "what will this spend produce?" or "what spend do I need for this target?"
  • Horizon flag when projection window captures less than 80% of the reinvestment curve
